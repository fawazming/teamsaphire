About Myself

My Name is Adeyemi Abdul-Hameed . I hailed from Sagamu in Ogun State. I am fair in complexion with a moderate height. I am currently studying Data Science at the Osun State University at Osogbo. The food I liked most is rice with stew and my best colour is Blue. I enjoyed reading Al-Quran and playing mobile games .

  • My educational journey started at Allah Akbar International School at Ajegunle, Sagamu. I did my Nursery and finished my Primary school there.

    I gained admission into Bade Unik Secondary School after passing the entrance examination. I chose to be in science class at the secondary level as I wanted to study a science related course in the tertiary institution. I did both WAEC and NECO there. I wrote JAMB in order to gain admission into a University.

    I was then offered an admission at the Osun State University to study Data Science so as to become a Data Analyst. I’m currently in my first year there and I’ve been enjoying the learning atmosphere there.

  • I attended madrasah to learn about Al-Quran and hadith. I learnt how to operate laptop and how to solve a rubik’s cube while I was at Bade Unik Secondary School.
