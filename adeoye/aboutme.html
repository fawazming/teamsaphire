My Educational Journey

My educational journey started at Allah Akbar International School at Ajegunle, Sagamu, where I completed my Nursery and Primary education.

After writing JAMB, I was offered admission at the Osun State University to study Data Science to become a Data Analyst. I’m currently in my first year and enjoying the learning atmosphere.
